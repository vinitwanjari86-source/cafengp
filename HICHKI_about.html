Hitchki, a renowned Bollywood-themed resto-bar, has established a vibrant presence in Nagpur, offering patrons a unique blend of nostalgic ambiance and innovative culinary delights. Located at Plot 2/1 & 11/2, 12th and Terrace Floor, Naniks Ashtavinayak Park Avenue, New Colony, Chaoni, Nagpur, Hitchki seamlessly fuses India's rich cinematic heritage with contemporary dining experiences. Ambiance and Theme Upon entering Hitchki, guests are immediately immersed in an environment that pays homage to Bollywood's golden era. The decor is thoughtfully curated, featuring vintage posters, iconic movie memorabilia, and subtle lighting that evokes a sense of nostalgia. This thematic setting not only appeals to movie enthusiasts but also provides a lively and engaging atmosphere for all visitors. The establishment's design encourages a relaxed and enjoyable dining experience, making it a popular choice for both casual outings and special occasions. Culinary Offerings
